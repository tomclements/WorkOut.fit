2. Workout style (split)

How you divide training across the week — independent of your goal below.

Full body hits every major area each session — great for 2–3 days/week.

Bro split: one body part per day (chest, back, legs, shoulders, arms) with higher volume. Best with 4–5 training days. Not the same as your training goal (muscle / strength / etc.).

3. Goal & experience

What you want from training — not which days or body parts you train.

4. How the plan gets harder
